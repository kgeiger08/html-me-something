My Weightloss Journey!

My Story

I have been overweight most of my life and struggled with eating healthy. Just last year around November 2019 I decided it was time to make a change. I was the heaviest I had ever been and if I wanted to have children I knew I needed to make a change. I wanted to be healthy and and feel better. At my heaviest simple tasks were now difficult and uncomfortable. So I ended up starting to make healthier choices and lost about 10lbs by January 2020. On January 12, 2020 I joined WW (Weight Watchers) and it has been life changing. Since January 2020 I have lost about 75lbs to date and feel so much better! I am not at my goal yet, but I am headed in the right direction. I'm so proud of myself and how far I have come and I am so very grateful for WW! It has been a wonderful lifestyle change and the WW community is so supportive!

"Don't give up on the person you are becoming" -Unknown

My Favorite Healthy Foods

One thing I love about WW is that you can still eat whatever you like just in smaller portions and in moderation. There are a lot of foods that I enjoy eating on a regular basis and recipes I like to cook often that are all healthy and nutritious. Here is a list of healthy foods I eat on a regular basis.

  • Homemade Tacos
  • Low Fat or Fat Free Cottage Cheese
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Fruit
  • Salads
  • Carrots with Hummus
  • Cucumbers
  • Turkey Sandwiches
